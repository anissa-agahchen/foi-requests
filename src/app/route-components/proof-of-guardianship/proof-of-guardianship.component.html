To make sure we give personal information about a child only to a person who has the right to access it, we need to verify your right to access the child’s records.

Do you have proof of guardianship of the child?

More information

What is proof of guardianship?

Proof of guardianship may include a court order, declaration form, or similar legal document. If you aren’t sure if you have the right documentation, visit our website to find out more.
