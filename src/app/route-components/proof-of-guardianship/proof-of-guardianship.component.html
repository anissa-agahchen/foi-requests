Do you have proof of guardianship of the child?

Do you have permission to access records on behalf of this person?

You must have proof of guardianship to access records about a child. If you don’t have it, please visit our website to view your options. You may complete and sign a Guardian Declaration Form in order to establish your right to access the requested records. You must have permission to access records on someone else’s behalf. Each ministry or agency has a unique form which you must fill out and have the person sign. For more information about authorization and consent, please review the resources on our website.

What is proof of guardianship?

Proof of guardianship may include a court order, declaration form, or similar legal document. If you aren’t sure if you have the right documentation, visit our website to find out more.
